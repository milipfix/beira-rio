360 Proposals for Entry

  • Technical workshop on Tech Transfer
  • Co-branded hackathon with social impact

Entry Channels

  • Letters of intent

  • Institutional participation via Inova RS or Caldeira

Tactical Steps (Months 1-6)

Month 1-2: Establishment and Credibility

Strategic presentation meetings with Jorge Audy and Pedro Valério. Participation in key ecosystem events.

Month 3-4: Co-creation and Demonstration

Pro-bono workshops and diagnostics. Institutional letters of intent with FIERGS and Porto Alegre City Hall.

Month 5-6: Consolidation and Expansion

Pilot projects and joint ventures. Positioning as a reference via Inova RS and FIERGS.
